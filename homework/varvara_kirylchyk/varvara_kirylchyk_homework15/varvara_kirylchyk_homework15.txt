Go to first line in file:  head -lines filename
Go to last line in file: tail -lines filename
Forward one window: CTRL+F
Forward one line: j
Backward one line: y
Search forward for matching line: /
Search backward for matching line: ?
Repeat previous search: Ctrl + P
Repeat previous search in reverse direction: Ctrl+R
